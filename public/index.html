FAQ
Common questions
What charts does it work with?+
Any chart screenshot works — TradingView, MetaTrader 4 & 5, Binance, Coinbase, Webull, or any broker platform. PNG and JPG files are supported. You can also paste directly with Ctrl+V.
What assets can it analyze?+
All major asset classes — crypto (BTC, ETH, SOL, etc.), forex (EUR/USD, GBP/USD, etc.), stocks (NVDA, AAPL, etc.), indices (SPX500, NASDAQ), and commodities (Gold, Oil). If it has a chart, the AI can read it.
How accurate is the AI?+
Our tracked A+ and A grade signals have historically achieved around 74% win rate. However, past performance does not guarantee future results. The AI is a tool to assist your analysis — not a guarantee. Always use proper risk management and never risk more than you can afford to lose.
What is the 4-pass engine?+
The 4-pass engine makes 4 separate AI calls per analysis: (1) Pure objective chart reading with zero bias, (2) Multi-timeframe alignment analysis, (3) Precision entry architecture, (4) Final verdict with 9 quality gates. This eliminates the confirmation bias that makes single-pass AI tools unreliable.
Can I cancel anytime?+
Yes — cancel anytime from your account settings. No contracts, no long-term commitments. You keep access until the end of your billing period.
Is this financial advice?+
No. PriceAction AI is an educational tool for analysis assistance only. It is not financial advice. Always do your own research, manage your risk appropriately, and never trade with money you cannot afford to lose.
