How It Works

Three steps from screenshot to signal.

1
Take a chart screenshot
Open any charting platform — TradingView, MT4, Binance, your broker — and take a screenshot of the chart you want analyzed. You can also paste directly with Ctrl+V or drag the file onto the analyzer.
2
Set your symbol and timeframe
Type the asset name (BTC, NVDA, EUR/USD, etc.) and select the timeframe of your chart. This helps the AI give you more context-aware analysis and correctly scale its level estimates.
3
Get your instant AI signal
Claude Vision reads the chart image, identifies candle patterns, trend structure, support/resistance, volume, and momentum indicators. Within seconds you get a BUY/SELL/HOLD verdict, confidence score, entry, stop loss, take profit, and a full written analysis.
